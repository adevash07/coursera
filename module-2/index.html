The underlying geological composition of soils has been reported to be a major source of heavy metal contamination of soil. The level of heavy metal in soils and the forms in which they exist are influenced by pedogenetic processes (Herawatiet al., 2000). Environmental contamination by heavy metals has become a world-wide problem during recent years due to the fact that heavy metals unlike some other pollutants are not biodegradable (Bazrafshanet al., 2015).Consequently, they are not detoxified but are bioaccumulated in the environment. Heavy metal refers to any metallic element whose density is relatively high and is toxic or poisonous even at low concentration (Inobemeet al., 2014).
